Yanfei Chen

Google

I am a Staff Research Engineer at Google Cloud AI Research. My work focuses on using large language models to solve practical problems, specifically in areas such as retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), tool learning, and LLM agents.

I'm a core contributor of Google Anti Money Laundering AI, Vertex AI Search, Gemini Enterprise Deep Research, and Vertex AI Agent Engine Memory Bank.

I earned my PhD in Industrial Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh, where I was advised by Professor Young Jae Chun. My doctoral research focused on structural modeling and analysis of implantable medical devices. I graduated with a BS in Electrical Engineering and Economics from Peking University.

Please email me if you are interested in working with me or joining our team.
