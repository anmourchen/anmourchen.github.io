I am a Research Engineer at Google Cloud AI Research. My work focuses on using LLMs to solve practical problems, specifically in areas such as retrieval augmented generation (RAG), tool learning, and LLM agents.

I earned my PhD in Industrial Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh, where I was advised by Professor Young Jae Chun. My doctoral research focused on structural modeling and analysis of implantable medical devices.

I graduated with a BS in Electrical Engineering and Economics from Peking University.
